About the organization

This organization was established in 2025 by graduate students that recognized the suffering of university students. The institution is soley built on empathy and bringing hope to the youth who are lost in pursuing useless degrees. The therapists we offer are dedicated teams of unemployed graduates who can help teens build their future with example.


Stay connected

Our program is offered throughout the world virtually to help young adults abroad take better care of their mental and physical wellbeing by venting regularly. Schedule your appointment from anywhere around the world and your venting session will be ready. Don't worry about the timezones, we are available at anytime of the day since everyone here has no other job. We will be waiting for your call.
